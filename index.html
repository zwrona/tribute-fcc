April Greiman

Thinker and an artist with a hybrid-based approach.

Here is a timeline of April Greiman's life

  • 1948 - Born in New York
  • 1970 - Accepted to the Basel School of Design
  • 1971 - Starts to persue her career as a teacher at Philadelphia College of Art
  • 1976 - Moves to LA and establishes the multidisciplinary practise Made in Space
  • 1982 - Makes a poster for 1984 Summer Olympic in Los Angeles
  • 1984 - The purches of her first Mackintosh computer
  • 1998 - Receives an AIGA medal
  • 1990 - publishes her first book "Hybrid Imaginery"

"one of the leading artists of the digital age, april greiman was among the first to embrace computer technology as a design tool, developing it in transmedia projects that yoke the rationality of european modernism with the disruptiveness of california new wave."
